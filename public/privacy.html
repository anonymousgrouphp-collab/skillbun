Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use the SkillBun Career Quiz, we ask for your Name, Degree/Program, and Current Year. This information is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage to personalize your quiz experience. We do not transmit or save this data to an external database.

2. External APIs (Gemini AI)

To generate personalized quiz questions and career roadmaps, your quiz responses are securely transmitted to the Google Gemini AI Engine. We only send your generalized quiz selections; no personally identifiable session data (like your name) is permanently stored by the AI provider outside of the context window.

3. Cookies & Tracking

SkillBun currently does not deploy intrusive third-party tracking cookies or analytical trackers. Your session is managed entirely on the client-side for privacy and speed.

4. Deleting Your Data

Because your session data is stored completely on your local device, you can delete all your personal data instantly by closing your browser session or clicking the "Logout" button inside the quiz dashboard profile dropdown.
