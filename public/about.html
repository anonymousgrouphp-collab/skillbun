About SkillBun

Last updated: February 26, 2026

SkillBun helps BCA, BSc, BS/BS-MS (AICS/CSDA), and B.Tech students discover practical tech career paths. We built it to reduce career confusion and convert guidance into concrete next steps.

What We Built

The platform now includes profile onboarding, an adaptive AI quiz, an AI counsellor chat (Bun-Bot), and career recommendations with roadmap links. Students can explore possible roles, compare paths, and continue learning through suggested next actions.

How We Operate

We use AI to generate personalized guidance while applying technical safeguards such as request validation, rate limiting, and human-verification controls. Where configured, profile data can also be synced securely to a Supabase-backed store to support continuity.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: make career direction clearer, faster, and more actionable for Indian tech students, without paywalls or unnecessary complexity.
